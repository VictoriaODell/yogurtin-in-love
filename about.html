About

A locally owned family establishment, Yogurt in Love first opened it's doors in December of 2012

Owners Ruth and George, son Nick Molina, and staff all work hard to provide San Marcos with a frozen yogurt shop that is dedicated to providing the best service to Hays County and all who visit.

Yogurt in Love prides itself in participating in many of the local events that keep San Marcos lively & unique. Often times the store can be found hosting events and specials for locals and visitors to enjoy.
